# Magazine (Destination Guide)

## Pre-IG Magazine
1. Magazine tab available in pre-trip bottom nav (before itinerary is generated)
2. Shows city cards for destination cities
3. Each city card triggers deep-dive loading on click

## Post-IG Magazine
4. Magazine tab shows in post-IG bottom nav as first tab
5. Each itinerary city has a section with highlights and deep dive
6. Deep dive sections: writeup, food specialties, weather, getting around, etiquette, did you know, more sights
7. Highlight cards (MagazineHighlightCard) show photo from Wikipedia, title, note
8. Photo area collapses when no Wikipedia photo found (no emoji placeholder)
9. Each highlight card has a Google Maps link
10. Items already in the itinerary show a ✓ badge
11. "Also in [destination]" section shows cities not on the itinerary
12. Deep dive data is cached at App level (survives tab switches)
13. Deep dive loading happens in background when trip opens
